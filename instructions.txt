Instructions

1.  This is 2 players game mode or player versus computer mode.
    Choose by pressing adequate key.

2.  The aim is to shoot all the enemy's ships by guessing their coordinates.

3.  Each player starts with placing 4 one-masted, 3 two-masted and 1 three-masted ship on the board. 
    To put a ship on the board define its coordinates. Two- and three- masted ships can be placed 
    vertically or horizontally.

4.  You shoot your enemy's ships by guessing their coordinates.

5.  Players take turns to shot the ships.

6.  Look at the battlefield while shooting. If you guess the same spot twice, you loose your bullet.

7.  Battle ends when the entire fleet of one of the player's is sunk.
